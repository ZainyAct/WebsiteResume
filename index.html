Publications

Integration of Machine Learning in Robotics (Ongoing)

This publication explores the symbiotic relationship between robotics and machine learning, delving into how advanced ML techniques enhance robotic systems' capabilities in perception, motion planning, and control. It showcases real-world applications where ML algorithms improve autonomy, adaptability, and efficiency in diverse robotic tasks.

Multi-Robot Collaboration and Coordination

This research investigates methodologies to orchestrate collaborative efforts among multiple robots, focusing on decentralized decision-making, task allocation, and cooperative control. It examines the advantages of employing multi-robot systems in dynamic environments, highlighting their scalability, robustness, and ability to achieve complex objectives efficiently.

Human-Robot Interaction and Collaboration

This publication explores the design and implementation of intuitive interfaces that facilitate seamless interaction between humans and robots in shared environments. It discusses techniques for understanding human commands, recognizing gestures, and inferring intentions, emphasizing the importance of user-centric design principles and user studies in creating effective human-robot collaboration systems.
